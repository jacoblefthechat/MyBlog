THE MISSION

Why I started this blog

I’m not a career coach, and I’m definitely not an HR "influencer." I’m someone who looked at the modern job market and realized the math doesn't add up anymore.

We were told that if we put in the time—4 years, 6 years, tens of thousands of dollars in tuition—we’d be rewarded with a career in our field. Instead, we graduated into a world of ghost jobs, automated rejection emails, and "entry-level" roles that require 5 years of experience.

Stop playing their game.

This blog isn't about writing a "better resume." It’s about understanding the systemic failure of the current hiring process and finding the back doors that actually work. I'm here to share the resources, the data, and the raw truth that the "experts" won't tell you.
